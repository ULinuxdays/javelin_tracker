Privacy & data

What we store, where it lives, and who can see it.

What we store

  • Athlete profiles (name, optional height/weight, team, notes).
  • Training sessions (date, event, best distance, throws, RPE, tags, notes).
  • Strength logs (date, exercise, load, reps, notes).
  • Coach accounts (name, email, hashed password, role).

Where itâ€™s stored

Data lives in the configured database for this deployment (see your admin). Backups may retain data for a limited time before rotation.

Who can see/edit

  • Head coach: full access to roster, sessions, teams, reports, imports/exports.
  • Assistant: log/edit sessions and athletes; no role or team management.
  • Trainer/S&C (future): read analytics; limited write to strength/notes.

Each deployment is intended for a single program; cross-program access is not provided.

Retention & removal

Data stays until your program owner removes it. Backups may persist for the retention window set by your host. For removal requests, contact your program admin.
