Recommended first week workflow

Keep it lightweight—just enough data to unlock readiness and load guidance.

  1. Day 1: Add your squad + log the day’s throws with RPE and notes.
  2. Day 3: Log at least one more session; tag conditions (wind/runway) and emphasis (tech/heavy).
  3. Day 5–7: Review Analytics “Load & readiness”; generate a weekly PDF and share with staff.
Tip: Consistent RPE + duration improves ACWR accuracy. Tag key cues to make reports more useful.
